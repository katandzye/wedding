FAQs

What You Need To Know

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at reidwedding2024@gmail.com

  • No Black. No White.

    NO EXCEPTIONS

    You will not be allowed into the Ceremony or Reception if you wear black or white. Absolutely no exceptions will be made. Again, you will be turned away.

  • What to Wear.

    Our Color Palette

    We strongly encourage you choose a color from our color palette! Please stay away from any colors too close to black or white.

  • Meals at the Venue.

    Prefix Meals.

    We will only have one meal available at our Wedding. If you have a special dietary restriction, please indicate so in your RSVP and contact us at our wedding email if you have further questions.

  • Where Can I Stay?

    Two Recommendations

    If you need a hotel for the weekend, we recommend two options. Either the Kokomo La Quinta (contact us to see if you can use our hotel block) or Courtyard Marriott.

  • Can I Bring My Child?

    TL;DR: NO

    We will not be allowing any children at either the ceremony or the reception. Please make other arrangements for your children that night.
